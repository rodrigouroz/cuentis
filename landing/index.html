What is Cuentis?

Cuentis is a chatbot designed to create personalized stories for children, making bedtime more magical and engaging. Just tell Cuentis about your child’s preferences, and it will weave a unique tale every time.

How It Works

Tell us about your child

Share some details about your child’s preferences, favorite characters, and more.

Get a unique story

Cuentis will generate a unique story tailored to your child’s interests.

Enjoy bedtime magic

Sit back, relax, and let your child be enchanted by a magical story!
